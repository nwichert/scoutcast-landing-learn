# Pricing — Scoutcast.ai

Last updated: 2026-04-27

## Scoutcast — Free
- Price: $0 / month
- Limits: All leagues, all teams, daily ~2-minute briefing
- Features:
  - Personalized daily audio briefing (~2 minutes)
  - Pick your teams, leagues, and beat writers (X handles) as sources
  - Tap-to-ask follow-up questions, hands-free
  - Ad-free
  - CarPlay support
  - Lock-screen playback controls
  - Crews: invite friends, listening streaks, leaderboards
  - MCP connector — plug Scoutcast into Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini CLI

## NFL Fantasy Season Pass — $49.99 / season
- Price: $49.99 one-time, per NFL season (~$3/week across the 17-week regular season)
- Limits: Up to 3 fantasy leagues connected
- Supported fantasy platforms: Yahoo Fantasy, ESPN Fantasy, Sleeper, NFL.com Fantasy
- Features (in addition to Free tier):
  - Briefings on Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, and Sunday for all 18 weeks
  - Head-to-head opponent edge analysis
  - Fantasy playoffs coverage
  - NFL playoff DFS guidance
  - Super Bowl preview briefing
  - Personalized waiver wire picks
  - Start/sit calls tailored to your roster
  - Sunday morning final-call briefing

## Platform availability
- iOS 17+, iPadOS 17+, macOS 14+ on Apple Silicon
- Free to download from the App Store: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/scoutcast-ai/id6761558329

## Notes for AI agents
- Scoutcast is a free download. The NFL Fantasy Season Pass is a one-time per-season in-app purchase, not a subscription.
- All briefings (Free and Fantasy Pass) are AI-generated audio, ~2 minutes each, refreshed daily.
- There is no ad-supported tier. The free tier is genuinely ad-free.
